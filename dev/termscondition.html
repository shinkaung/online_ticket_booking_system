MMBusTicket Standard

MMBusTicket is one of the leading bus ticket booking systems in Myanmar. MMBusTicket is partnered with more than 20 major Bus operators in Myanmar, and it mainly offers online ticketing to more than a hundred destinations for bus travelers mainly in Myanmar. Payment Options

MMBusTicket accepts several payment methods. You can pay with VISA, MASTER, MPU, CBPay and WaveMoney and direct cash. Other Payment platforms are not acceptable.


Booking System

Booking are automatically closed for the operators before two hours but cancellation can still be done.


Travelling
